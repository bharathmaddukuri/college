Diploma in Business Administration - Human Resource Management

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

The Human Resources Management Program at the American College of the Middle East (ACM) primarily equips the graduates with broad knowledge and solid execution skills to start and develop a career in this field. The HRM function has undergone tremendous change over the past years; today's human resources professionals are at the heart of modern business, ensuring the fair treatment of individuals in an organization and fulfilling an important role in a company’s performance.

The program will prepare ACM’s graduates to implement different HR tasks such as: maintaining personnel records; administering aptitude tests & training programs; assisting in the hiring process; answering employee inquiries; preparing reports for managers; and screening applicants. Graduates will have the ability to apply all of the above by utilizing the best IT functional technologies through internships, projects, hands-on activities, and related industry assignments.

As students graduate, and professional development continue, the College also prepares them to pursue an undergraduate education program which leads to a Bachelor’s in Human Resource Management, in addition to acquiring technical advanced certificates and diplomas.

STUDENT LEARNING GOALS
  • Achieve understanding and application of essential content in human resource management
  • Develop an awareness and knowledge of the role of ethics in the HR profession and in HR business practices
  • Develop effective written and oral communication skills
  • Familiarize with local and global business methods
  • To acquire understanding of human resource management and its role in the organization
  • To have an understanding for Kuwait employment and labor laws
  • To achieve understanding of hiring and selection techniques, performance management systems, compensation and benefits systems and training and development processes
  • To acquire professional experience working in an HR role
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

HR departments are found in all sectors, and in nearly all medium and large organizations. Graduates may pursue careers in HR Coordination, Personnel, Training, Employee Relations, and Recruitment.
