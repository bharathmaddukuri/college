Office of registrar

The Registrar’s Office is responsible for keeping all student records and ensuring the accuracy and integrity of such records. It serves as the College's central service and academic support unit to:

  • Conduct and manage processes related to registration.
  • Record student and instructional academic program information.
  • Coordinate and produce official enrollment and academic certification from academic records.
  • Provide support for determining student’s academic qualifications and academic progress.
  • Manage Faculty Workload.
