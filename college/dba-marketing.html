Diploma in Business Administration - Marketing

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

The study of Marketing is an exciting field that introduces students to all aspects of marketing, from product conception to product consumption, thus opening students up to countless opportunities in the industry. Tailored to blend creative applied knowledge with a grounding of business, marketing and contextual studies, it provides students with career preparation for entry and mid-level positions in marketing.

The program gives students appropriate knowledge and skills to understand the function of marketing in the firm and in society by introducing students to the fundamentals of marketing and how to implement marketing plans. Courses are designed to develop students’ abilities to analyze, plan, implement, and control the creation of demand among targeted buyers. This program of study encompasses a wide range of marketing activities, such as advertising, packaging, promotion, and distribution.

As students graduate, and professional development continue, the College also prepares them to pursue an undergraduate education program which leads to a Bachelor’s in Marketing, in addition to acquiring technical advanced certificates and diplomas.

STUDENT LEARNING GOALS
  • To achieve depth of understanding and application of essential content in marketing
  • Develop competency to develop, measure, analyze, validate and communicate business information
  • Develop effective written and oral communication skills.
  • Develop the leadership and teamwork skills to function effectively in a diverse cross-functional environment.
  • Develop an awareness and knowledge of the role of ethics in the marketing profession and in marketing business practices
  • Familiarize with local and global business methods
  • To develop an understanding of what marketing/marketing management is and what decisions are involved in regards to the marketing process, market segmentation/positioning and marketing mix
  • To understand the responsibilities and tasks of a marketing manager
  • To understand marketing planning processes, and customer relationship management.
  • To acquire professional experience working in a marketing role
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Marketing departments are found in nearly all medium and large organizations. Graduates may pursue careers in Marketing Coordination, Advertising, Promotion, Public Relations, and Product Development.
