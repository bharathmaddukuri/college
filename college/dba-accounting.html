Diploma in Business Administration - Accounting

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

The Accounting Program at the American College of the Middle East (ACM) is committed to provide students with the general educational and specific technical knowledge necessary to enter the accounting profession and pursue a successful professional career.

We at ACM believe that in an increasingly complex business environment, a successful accountant must possess broad knowledge of the business field to be able to utilize sound and professional judgment. An accountant must have essential business skills to communicate verbally and in writing, as well as the ability to work in teams, motivate others, and manage people and tasks in an organized and well-planned method. Consequently, the program aims to arm its students with knowledge of basic accounting concepts, accounting applications, and recognized accounting business practices.

As students graduate, and professional development continues, the College also prepares them to pursue an undergraduate education program which leads to a Bachelorâ€™s in Accounting, in addition to acquiring technical advanced certificates and diplomas.

STUDENT LEARNING GOALS
  • Achieve understanding and application of essential content in accounting
    • To attain a level of understanding and application related to financial and managerial accounting
    • Develop competency to develop, measure, analyze, validate and communicate financial and other information
  • Develop an awareness and knowledge of the role of ethics in the accounting profession and in accounting business practices
  • Develop effective written and oral communication skills
  • Develop the leadership and teamwork skills to function effectively in a diverse cross-functional environment
  • Familiarize with local business methods and accounting standards
  • To acquire professional experience working in an accounting role
  • Understand current business technologies and software in essential tasks
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Students pursuing an accounting concentration at ACM are offered challenging and rewarding opportunities in accounting and auditing, in both, government and private commercial enterprises. Graduates may pursue careers in Administrative Management, Office Management, Administrative Support, and Customer Service.
