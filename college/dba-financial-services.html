Diploma in Business Administration - Financial Services

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

The objective of the Financial Services Program at the American College of the Middle East (ACM) is to develop the foundation for individuals to become professional financial services practitioners. They will be able to build practices in financial planning, investments & securities, and treasury management.

The program is designed to provide an overview of the various financial services organizations, their service offerings, and the essential role they play within the economy. Our curriculum is built on the solid synergy of various business theories and financial techniques. Students will gain applied knowledge and on-the-job skills through internships, projects, hands-on activities, and related industry assignments.

As students graduate, and professional development continue, the College also prepares them to pursue an undergraduate education program which leads to a Bachelorâ€™s in Finance, in addition to acquiring technical advanced certificates and diplomas.

STUDENT LEARNING GOALS
  • To achieve depth of understanding and application of essential content in finance
  • Acquire professional experience working in a finance role
  • Develop effective written and oral communication skills
  • Develop the leadership and teamwork skills to function effectively in a diverse cross-functional environment
  • Develop an awareness and knowledge of the role of ethics in the financial profession and in finance and investment business practices
  • Students will be prepared to analyze a situation in the financial services and assess possible solutions
  • Students will understand the role of financial policy and investment analysis
  • Students will learn the dynamics of stock markets, asset valuation, portfolio construction and risk management
  • Use analytical and quantitative skills to analyze and formulate business decisions
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Graduates will be well suited to work in financial services jobs in a variety of financial organizations including banks, investment companies, and insurance companies. They will also be able to apply their skills to develop a successful independent financial services practice.
