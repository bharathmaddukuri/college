New Student Orientation for Fall 2014-2015 Semester

The American College of the Middle East would like to welcome all the new students for the Fall semester of the academic year 2014 - 2015.

When?

Monday 8 September, 2014 for males
Tuesday 9 September, 2014 for females

A message will be sent to each student with the appointed date and time for each group.

Where?

ACM building. You can park at Gate H

What do I bring with me?

  • The Original Civil ID
  • License plate number for the car that you will use to enter the college parking area during the academic year (up to 2 cars)

Why shall I attend the orientation?

It's important for you to attend the orientation since you will get essential information to help you to have a smooth academic semester. The orientation schedule will cover the following:

  • Taking a picture of you and receiving a student ID card
  • Registering your car and receiving a car sticker (up to 2 cars)
  • Attending sessions to help you familiarize with the College rules and regulations, learning how to use the College E-mail address, checking course schedule online, etc.
  • Receiving important forms: PUC Allowance and College and PUC acknowledgments

Undergraduate Program: Tuesday 23 September, 2014

You are requested to attend individually and arrive on time.

Looking forward to seeing you at the orientation day and wishing you a successful academic year.
