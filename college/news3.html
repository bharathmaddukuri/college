ACM Street Festival Event

The ACM Business and Engineering Societies organized an outdoor exhibition event on the 17th and 18th of December to showcase the businesses of local entrepreneurs. The two-day event was hosted by television personality Hessa Al- Loughani, who took the stage and ran friendly competitions that included Best Booth and Best Business Idea. The crowd was also entertained with a guitar performance by a group of talented ACM students, which contributed to creating a full, fun and eventful day.
